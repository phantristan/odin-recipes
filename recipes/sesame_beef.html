Sesame Beef

Description

Strips of steak and chopped scallions are marinated with soy sauce and garlic, then quickly stir fried with sesame seeds. Add a little sesame oil to the marinade and serve with rice.

Ingredients
  • 4 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 3 teaspoons minced garlic
  • 1 diced onion
  • 1 diced green bell pepper
  • 1 tablespoon chili flakes
  • 2 tablespoons sesame seeds
  • green onions to garnish
  • beef of choice
Steps
  1. Add the beef to the marinade created with the soy sauce, sugar, sesame oil, and minced garlic (overnight is the best option, the longer the better).
  2. When done marinating, heat a pan and cook the onions + bell pepper
  3. After vegetables are cooked through, add the beef + marinade and mix with the vegetables.
  4. When the beef is almost cooked through, add the sesame seeds + chili flakes and cook for 2 more minutes. Serve.
